About Mossbridge

Mossbridge is an environmental data company based in Portland, Oregon, operating since 2019. The company provides sensor-based environmental monitoring, automated sustainability reporting, and carbon footprint analysis for mid-size businesses and local governments in the Pacific Northwest.

Services

Environmental Monitoring

Continuous sensor-based monitoring of air quality, water discharge, noise levels, and emissions at client facilities.

Sustainability Reporting

Automated generation of sustainability reports aligned with GRI Standards and CDP frameworks.

Carbon Footprint Analysis

Scope 1 and Scope 2 greenhouse gas emissions analysis based on metered operations data.
