Mossbridge Analytics is an environmental data company based in Portland, Oregon, operating since 2019.

The company provides sensor-based environmental monitoring, automated sustainability reporting, and carbon footprint analysis for mid-size businesses and local governments in Oregon, Washington, and Northern California.

Data is collected through on-site IoT sensors deployed at client locations and processed through the Mossbridge platform. Reports are delivered monthly or on-demand through a web dashboard.
