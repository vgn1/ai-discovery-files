## Products & Services

### Q4: How does the environmental monitoring work?

**Answer:**
Mossbridge installs IoT sensors at client facilities that continuously measure air quality, water discharge, noise levels, and emissions. Data is transmitted in real time to the Mossbridge platform, where it's accessible through a web dashboard.

**Source:** https://www.mossbridge-analytics.example.com/services/monitoring/

### Q5: What sustainability reporting frameworks do you support?

**Answer:**
Reports align with GRI (Global Reporting Initiative) Standards and CDP (formerly Carbon Disclosure Project) frameworks. Reports are generated monthly and can be exported as PDF or CSV for regulatory submissions.

**Source:** https://www.mossbridge-analytics.example.com/services/reporting/
